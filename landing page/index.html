Stay at Our Luxury 5-Star Hotel in Surabaya, Indonesia

Perfectly located near Tunjungan Plaza Mall, JW Marriott Hotel Surabaya invites you to experience the height of five-star hospitality. Relax in 407 beautifully appointed rooms, including 22 extended-stay apartments, and enjoy effortless access to the city’s shopping, culinary, and business destinations.

From bespoke concierge services to curated dining, every touchpoint is designed to make your stay more memorable.
